OUR MISSION

To provide students with an academically superior education founded upon Biblical principles which trains students to live with purpose and integrity.

PROGRAMS

At Life Academy, our approach to education is grounded in biblical truth and academic excellence. We use the Apostolic Bible Curriculum, a Spirit-led program designed to help students build a strong foundation in the Apostolic doctrine. This curriculum is based on the teachings of respected leaders like Bishop Joel Holmes and Bishop Larry Booker. It helps students grow in their understanding of the Bible, develop Christian character, and walk in spiritual maturity. Our goal is to teach, disciple, and equip students to live boldly and faithfully for Christ. We also incorporate the Abeka curriculum, a trusted Christian academic program for Pre-K through 12th grade. Created by Abeka Book, a ministry of Pensacola Christian College, this curriculum supports high academic achievement while reinforcing a biblical worldview across every subject.
